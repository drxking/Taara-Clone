Humans thrive on connection.
Yet billions lack reliable connectivity. This gap demands a groundbreaking solution. Taara lights the way forward, delivering blazing fast, abundant connectivity.

Introducing
Taara Lightbridge

Taara uses light to extend and amplify the global communications network. From under-connected communities to densely populated cities, our technology bridges existing gaps to amplify the connectivity infrastructure.

SPEED

Connect farther and faster.

Using wireless optical communications, Taara Lightbridge can achieve speeds of up to 20 Gbps over distances of up to 20 km – and lower latency than fiber.

Setup

Make installation light work.

Installation is completed in hours, not days – all without digging, spectrum licensing or right-of-way permitting.

Stability

Keep the connection.

When atmospheric conditions disrupt the light, our adaptive rate and hybrid architecture maintains the connection, with minimal downtime.

Security

Stay safe and secure.

Connections are kept secure, private, and continually protected against threats.
